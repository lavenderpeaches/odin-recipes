Pink Sauce Pasta

Description

This delightful pasta dish is a culinary treat that combines the sweetness of tomatoes with the richness of cream, resulting in a captivating pink sauce. Its vibrant color is complemented by the tender pasta, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. This versatile meal is perfect for a quick and satisfying lunch or a light dinner.

The creamy pink sauce, infused with the aromatic essence of garlic and onion, coats the pasta perfectly. A generous sprinkling of Parmesan cheese adds a salty and nutty undertone, elevating the dish to new heights. This pasta is not only visually appealing but also a delightful experience for the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet spaghetti pasta
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions, drain, and toss with olive oil.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Saut√© onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook until slightly softened.
  4. Stir in cream and cook until combined.
  5. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat.
  6. Stir in Parmesan cheese.
  7. Serve pasta in bowls, garnished with parsley.
