Idli Sambhar

Description

Idli Sambar is a classic South Indian breakfast duo that has captured hearts and taste buds for generations. Soft, fluffy idlis are the perfect accompaniment to the tangy, flavorful sambar, a lentil-based vegetable stew brimming with aromatic spices. Together, they create a symphony of tastes and textures that is both comforting and satisfying.

The sambar, a South Indian culinary gem, is a complex blend of vegetables, lentils, and a medley of spices that come together to create a truly unforgettable taste experience. The addition of tempering, a process of sautéing spices in hot oil, infuses the sambar with an extra layer of depth and complexity. This breakfast combination is a wholesome and nutritious way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Idli batter
  • Spinach
  • Drumstick leaves
  • Eggplant
  • Green chili
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Dried red chili
  • Curry leaves
  • Water

Steps

  1. Ferment idli batter and steam idlis.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, splutter mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and dried red chili.
  3. Add chopped onion, garlic, and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add chopped spinach, drumstick leaves, eggplant, and green chili.
  5. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  6. Pour water and bring to a boil.
  7. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot sambar with idlis.
