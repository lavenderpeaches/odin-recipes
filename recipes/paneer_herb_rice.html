Paneer Herb Rice

Description

This vibrant and flavorful bowl is a perfect blend of Indian and Mediterranean cuisines. Fragrant basmati rice forms the base for a medley of grilled paneer cubes marinated in Indian spices. A cooling and refreshing herb sauce made with Greek yogurt, mint, and cilantro adds a delightful tanginess to the dish. Topped with a colorful assortment of fresh vegetables like bell peppers, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion, this bowl is not only delicious but also incredibly healthy. It's a light and satisfying meal that is perfect for a quick and nutritious lunch or dinner.

The combination of flavors and textures creates a truly enjoyable eating experience. The crispy paneer contrasts beautifully with the soft rice, while the fresh vegetables provide a refreshing crunch. The herb sauce ties all the elements together, resulting in a bowl that is both comforting and invigorating.

Ingredients

  • cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the rice: Rinse the rice thoroughly. In a saucepan, combine rice, water, and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-18 minutes or until cooked through. Fluff with a fork and drizzle with olive oil.
  2. Prepare the paneer: In a bowl, combine paneer cubes with cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and salt. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook paneer cubes until golden brown on all sides.
  3. Make the herb sauce: In a small bowl, combine Greek yogurt, mint, cilantro, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Assemble the bowl: Divide cooked rice into bowls. Top with grilled paneer, mixed bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Drizzle with herb sauce.
