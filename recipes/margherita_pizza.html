Margherita Pizza

Description

The Margherita pizza is a timeless Italian classic that celebrates simplicity and fresh ingredients. Its minimalist approach allows the quality of the components to shine through. A thin, crispy crust is the foundation for a generous layer of tangy tomato sauce, topped with the creamy goodness of mozzarella cheese.

Fresh basil leaves, a vibrant green garnish, add a touch of herbaceousness to the pizza. Drizzled with a high-quality olive oil and sprinkled with sea salt, the Margherita pizza delivers a perfect balance of flavors and textures. It is a dish that embodies the essence of Italian cuisine: fresh, simple, and undeniably delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce evenly on pizza base.
  2. Top with small pieces of mozzarella cheese.
  3. Arrange basil leaves, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle salt.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown.
  5. Remove from oven and serve immediately.
